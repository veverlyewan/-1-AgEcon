About

Ag stands for Agriculture, Econ stands for Economics

The AgEcon Portal is dedicated to fueling the professional growth and knowledge of faculty members within the Department of Agriculture and Economics (DA&E). Established in response to your need for a streamlined and centralized resource, this web platform bridges the gap between faculty development initiatives and readily accessible learning opportunities.
